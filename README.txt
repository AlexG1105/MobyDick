Welcome to our ENGLISH 1102 Project!

How to play:
Your goal is to travel to islands (dock at their ports) and fight bosses by shooting at them. Don't get hit by too many bullets, or you'll die!

Controls:
WASD - movement
Click or space - shoot
Click - interact

Tips:
USE A MOUSE (higly recommended)
READ THE TEXT (it gives you info about the story and where to go)
DODGE BOSS BULLETS
